There was a rumor that you can build games, like, 2D Pixel Adventure RPGs. And then we found a tilset from FF3, and it was all downhill from there.

What... is this...

This is a random forest! With a shiny, blond avatar hero! (S)he has no weapons to speak of and desperately wants to leave this confusing land.

Controls:

You can control the avatar located in the upper-left corner with Arrow Keys on your Keyboard. We do not condone stroking the screen in an effort to make things happen. Because nothing will happen.

Hint You can walk behind trees!
