HelloGame

There was a rumor that you can build games, like, 2D Pixel Adventure RPGs. And then we found a tilset from FF3, and it was all downhill from there.

What... is this...

This is a random forest! You can navigate it with your impossibly small avatar straight out of the NES era. Trees et al courtesy of Lorestrome Pixel Archive.

Controls:

You can control the tiny 16x16 pixel blue smudge in the top left corner with the Arrow Keys on your Keyboard.

We do not condone stroking the screen in an effort to make things happen. Because nothing will happen.

Hint: You can walk behind trees.
